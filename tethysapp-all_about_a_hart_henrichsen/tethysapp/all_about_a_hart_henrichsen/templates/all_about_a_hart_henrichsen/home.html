This app is all about Alexander Hart Henrichsen.

Early Life

Hart was born on October 20th, 1996 in Altus Oklahoma Air Force Base. A year and a half later, he moved with his family to Monteray California. Here he has his first memories, namely, the monteray aquarium, the fish market, and a rabbit named 'poopers' that he watched. After four years, His parents moved to San Angaleo and then San Antonio with the Air force. Shortly before the housing colaspe in 2008, Hart's father, Richard, found a new job in Washington D.C. The family moved to Maryland, where Hart Lived until graduation.

Schooling

Hart attending 5 elementary schools due to his family moving so often. He attended Southern Middle school where he started playing the Oboe. He graduated from Patuxent High School in the top 10% of his class. He was one of the founders of the school's robotics club. He was also served leadership positions in marching band, math club, and programing groups. In 2014 Hart was accepted into Brigham Young University. He completed a year of Education before going on his Mission. He is majoring in Civil and Environmental Engineering and minoring in Mathematics.

Mission

Hart served his mission from July 2015 to June 2017. He served in the Belgium/Netherlands Mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. Here he learned Dutch. He served in Spijknesse, Nijmege, Delft, Rotterdam, and Leiden in the Netherlands and Geel in Belgium. He was companions with 11 companions from Utah, Wisconsin, Suriname, Washington, England, New Mexico, and Jamaica. He served as co-district leader, senior-co-district leader, and trainer. 3 converts were baptized while he was serving in their Areas. Two are still active. Hart also helped in the prevention of multiple suicides while serving his mission. He was known by a stake president and a few others as Elder Hart even though his title was officially Elder Henrichsen.
